Error Codes :::

Status Frame ::
Byte Index 6 : State/Initialization Mode/Stage/Message etc.
0x00	|	Idle
0x01	|	Priming Mode
0x02	|	Fault/Error
0x03	|	Reminder/Message/Information
.
.
.
.


Byte Index 11 : Reminder/Message/Error codes.
Was 	0x13 while machine was in Priming Mode.			|	Index 6 Byte = 0x01.
Became  0x28 when Set Time Message(Code 40) was there.	|	Index 6 Byte - 0x03(Reminder/Message/Information).
Became	0x00 when the Time was Set.						|	Index 6 Byte - 0x00(Idle).
Became	0x1E when Heater Too Hot Error(Code 30) came.	|	Index 6 Byte - 0x02(Error).
